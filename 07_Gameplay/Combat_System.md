# Combat System

## Core rules
- Real-time combat with unit formations and hero abilities.
- Clear attack, armor, and damage classes.
- Positioning and terrain matter.

## Key mechanics
- Facing and formation bonuses.
- Morale for standard troops.
- Setup time for siege.
- Status effects: rust, burn, slow, blind, corruption.
- Friendly collision for heavy units only.

## Combat priorities
1. Readability.
2. Counterplay.
3. Short tactical windows.
4. Limited ability spam.

## Retreat
Units may retreat manually. Some commanders improve retreat speed or reduce morale loss.

## Death and recovery
Heroes become unavailable temporarily in skirmish and may be revived at faction-specific structures.
