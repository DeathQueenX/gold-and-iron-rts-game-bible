# Map Control and Objectives

## Design intent

Gold and Iron RTS should reward active map control. A player who only turtles should eventually lose access to resources, relics, ports or strategic vision.

## Objective categories

### Resource nodes
Resource nodes are capturable or harvestable points that create conflict lanes.

Examples:
- Iron Mine;
- Coal Seam;
- Sunstone Shrine;
- Coral Reef;
- Salt Flat;
- Abyssal Rift.

### Strategic structures
Neutral or capturable structures that alter battlefield control.

Examples:
- Watchtower;
- Signal Beacon;
- Neutral Dock;
- Mountain Gate;
- Delta Bridge Control;
- Trade Post.

### Relic sites
High-risk PvE objectives guarded by creeps or bosses.

Examples:
- Star-Forge Ruin;
- Sunken Coral Vault;
- Obsidian Sentinel Gate;
- Ancient Solar Mirror.

### Route control
Routes are not just visuals. They should affect movement and economy.

Examples:
- Ferrum Iron Road;
- Aurelian Imperial Road;
- Thalassian Trade Lane;
- Ashlands Hazard Trail.

## Capture mechanics

### Standard capture
A unit must remain in a capture radius without enemy contest. Workers capture faster, heroes capture slower but can hold area better.

### Fortified capture
Some objectives require destroying neutral guards or disabling a structure first.

### Ritual capture
Magic objectives require a caster or hero to channel.

## Objective rewards

| Objective | Reward |
|---|---|
| Watchtower | Vision |
| Trade Post | Coin over time |
| Mountain Gate | Path control |
| Sunstone Shrine | Sunstone + solar buff |
| Coral Reef | Coral + naval unlock |
| Abyssal Rift | Abyssal Crystal + corruption risk |
| Star-Forge Ruin | Relic tech or boss reward |

## Anti-snowball rules

- Captured objectives should be contestable.
- Defensive advantage should exist but not be absolute.
- Some objectives should decay if not defended.
- High-value objectives should create visible map alerts.

## Faction interaction

### Ferrum
Wants mines, chokepoints and gates.

### Aurelia
Wants shrines, roads and vision structures.

### Thalassia
Wants ports, trade posts and coastal objectives.

### Ashlands
Corrupts objectives and transforms rewards into high-risk versions.
