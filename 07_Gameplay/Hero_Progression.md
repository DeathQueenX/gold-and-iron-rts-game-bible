# Hero Progression

## Match progression
- Heroes gain experience from combat, objectives, and boss encounters.
- Level cap recommendation: 10.
- Ability points unlock at levels 2, 4, 6, 8, and 10.

## Campaign progression
- Persistent relic slot.
- Limited carry-over items.
- Branch-specific upgrades.
- Relationship and faction choices may unlock alternate abilities.

## Design rules
- No hero should scale into a one-unit army.
- Support heroes scale through army synergy.
- Ultimate abilities unlock late and require clear telegraphing.
- Respec is allowed between campaign acts.
