# Victory Conditions

## Standard
Destroy all enemy command structures or force surrender.

## Objective victory
Hold designated strategic objectives for a fixed duration.

## Economic victory
Maintain superior trade or resource control until the victory threshold is reached.

## Relic victory
Capture and stabilize a major relic site while preventing enemy disruption.

## Crisis survival
Survive Ashlands waves and defeat the final boss.

## Campaign-specific
Mission objectives may include escort, defense, sabotage, extraction, or ritual completion.

## Rules
- Alternative victories must be clearly announced.
- Opponents need time and tools to contest them.
- Ranked 1v1 should default to standard victory.
