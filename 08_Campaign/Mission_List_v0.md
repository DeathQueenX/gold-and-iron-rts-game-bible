# Mission List v0

## Purpose

Această listă definește prima versiune a campaniei RTS. Fiecare misiune introduce sau combină sisteme de gameplay.

## Act I — Border of Gold and Iron

### Mission 01 — The Weight of Gold

**Playable faction:** Aurelia

**Objective:** Introduce basic camera, selection, movement, small combat and Solar Guard.

**Core mechanic:** Formation discipline.

**Secondary objective:** Protect Sun Mason workers repairing an outpost.

### Mission 02 — Whispering Gorge

**Playable faction:** Aurelia, then Ferrum encounter.

**Objective:** Survive border ambush and retreat through canyon paths.

**Core mechanic:** Fog of war and ambush detection.

**Secondary objective:** Recover sealed dispatches from abandoned tents.

### Mission 03 — Iron and Ash

**Playable faction:** Ferrum

**Objective:** Secure the captured supply line and return to Dreadfast.

**Core mechanic:** Heavy infantry, chokepoint fighting.

**Secondary objective:** Keep at least 70% of Iron Guard alive.

### Mission 04 — Dreadfast Must Hold

**Playable faction:** Ferrum

**Objective:** Build base defenses and repel solar siege.

**Core mechanic:** Workers, buildings, towers, repairs.

**Secondary objective:** Save outer mining families for bonus workers.

## Act II — Storm and Tide

### Mission 05 — Edge of the Shroud

**Playable faction:** Ferrum

**Objective:** Reach the Southern Delta under ash storms.

**Core mechanic:** Environmental hazards.

### Mission 06 — Sea of Glass

**Playable faction:** Thalassia

**Objective:** Establish blockade and protect trade ships.

**Core mechanic:** Naval movement and trade lanes.

### Mission 07 — Coral Gates

**Playable faction:** Infiltration squad / hero mission

**Objective:** Enter Aethelgard through trade identity and avoid full-scale battle.

**Core mechanic:** Limited units, stealth zones, scripted patrols.

### Mission 08 — Iron Tide

**Playable faction:** Ferrum

**Objective:** Break the blockade with prototype ironclads.

**Core mechanic:** Mixed naval/land combat.

## Act III — Ashlands Wake

### Mission 09 — Outpost of Shivers

**Playable faction:** Ferrum + Aria support

**Objective:** Establish foothold in the north and survive Heart Pulse.

**Core mechanic:** Corruption zones and containment.

### Mission 10 — The Star-Forge

**Playable faction:** Mixed alliance

**Objective:** Defeat guardians and extract relic.

**Core mechanic:** Boss phases.

### Mission 11 — The Engine of Light

**Playable faction:** Aurelia

**Objective:** Protect or sabotage solar engine depending campaign branch.

**Core mechanic:** Super-weapon charging.

### Mission 12 — Heart of Fire

**Playable faction:** Alliance

**Objective:** Final multi-phase boss against Heart Avatar.

**Core mechanic:** Multi-front defense, boss mechanics, purge/seal choice.

## Mission design checklist

- Each mission has one primary new system.
- Each mission has at least one optional objective.
- Each mission has a unique map biome.
- Each mission rewards a unit, tech, hero level or campaign choice.
