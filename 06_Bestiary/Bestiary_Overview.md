# Bestiary Overview

## Purpose

Bestiarul oferă creaturi pentru campanie, creep camps, boss fights și evenimente dinamice pe hartă.

Creaturile nu trebuie tratate ca simple unități neutre. Ele trebuie să spună ceva despre biome, magie și istoria lumii.

## Creature categories

### Natural beasts
Faună locală, folosită pentru early creep camps și obiective mici.

Examples:
- Mountain wolves;
- Ash boars;
- Delta serpents;
- Reef lurkers.

### Mutated beasts
Creaturi afectate de Abyssal Rust, folosite pentru mid-game pressure.

Examples:
- Rust Beast;
- Ironhide Stalker;
- Ashmaw Brute;
- Caldera Hound.

### Ancient guardians
Entități care păzesc ruine, relicve sau structuri vechi.

Examples:
- Star-Forge Guardian;
- Obsidian Sentinel;
- Sunken Coral Warden;
- Solar Mirror Automaton.

### Boss entities
Creaturi mari, construite ca obiective de hartă.

Examples:
- Abyssal Colossus;
- Heart Avatar;
- Tidal Leviathan;
- The Rusted Titan.

## Creep camp design

### Camp tier 1
- 2-4 creaturi mici;
- reward: coin, minor item, small resource crate.

### Camp tier 2
- 1 mini-boss + 3 guards;
- reward: resource node, relic fragment, experience.

### Camp tier 3
- boss regional;
- reward: map control, powerful item, unique tech unlock.

## Debuffs

### Rust Stack
Reduce armor and repair efficiency. At max stacks, mechanical units may briefly jam.

### Ash Blindness
Reduces vision and ranged accuracy.

### Deep Chill
Slows movement and attack speed in Ashlands.

### Tide Drag
Pulls units toward water or slows movement near coast.

## Boss fight rules

- Bosses must telegraph abilities.
- Bosses should change phases.
- Boss rewards must be strategic, not only stat bonuses.
- Bosses can create temporary alliances in campaign.
