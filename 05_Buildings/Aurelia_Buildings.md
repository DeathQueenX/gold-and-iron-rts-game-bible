# Aurelia Buildings

## Solar Forum

### Role
Main command building, worker production, Authority generation.

### Gameplay
The Solar Forum is the center of order. It should encourage structured base layouts connected by Imperial Roads.

### Upgrades
- Forum of Law — increases Authority generation.
- Twin Sun Emblem — unlocks advanced decrees.
- Marble Stores — increases Coin and Sunstone capacity.

## Radiant Barracks

### Role
Primary infantry production.

### Trains
- Solar Guard;
- Sun Spearman;
- Radiant Archer;
- Dawnblade after tech.

### Upgrades
- Imperial Drills;
- Radiant Armor;
- Formation Command.

## Sunstone Chapel

### Role
Resource and magic support structure.

### Gameplay
Improves Sunstone gathering and acts as a minor purification node.

## Weaver Academy

### Role
Caster production and magical upgrades.

### Trains
- Light Weaver;
- High Weaver.

### Research
- Radiant Shields;
- Purity Rites;
- Starlight Refraction.

## Crystal Yard

### Role
Crystal constructs and siege support.

### Produces
- Sunstone Ballista;
- Crystal Sentinel.

## Dawn Tower

### Role
Defensive tower with solar beam attack.

### Strength
High precision, good against light units and corrupted targets.

### Weakness
Lower HP than Ferrum towers and reduced efficiency in fog.

## Solar Mirror Array

### Role
Tier 3 super-tech structure.

### Function
Improves vision, solar artillery accuracy and late-game decrees.

## Imperial Archive

### Role
Research building focused on Authority, roads and command systems.

### Unlocks
- Road Authority;
- Imperial Mandate;
- Solar Logistics.
