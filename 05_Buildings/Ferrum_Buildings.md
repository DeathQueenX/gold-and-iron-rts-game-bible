# Ferrum Buildings

## Iron Hall

### Role
Main command building, worker production, tier upgrades.

### Gameplay
The Iron Hall should feel like a fortress even before walls are built. It anchors Ferrum's defensive identity.

### Upgrades
- Hall Reinforcement — increases HP and armor.
- Dominion Muster — unlocks faster infantry rally.
- Deep Vaults — increases Iron storage.

## Forge Yard

### Role
Primary infantry production.

### Trains
- Ash Militia;
- Iron Guard;
- Forge Pike;
- Rustbreaker after tech.

### Upgrades
- Hardened Plate;
- Pike Bracing;
- Veteran Drills.

## Great Furnace

### Role
Core technology and Forge Heat production.

### Mechanics
Generates Forge Heat over time. Can spend Forge Heat to accelerate production or temporarily buff defenses.

### Risk
If overused, nearby production structures can suffer Overheat, pausing production briefly.

## Coal Silo

### Role
Resource storage and steam tech support.

### Gameplay
Coal Silos increase stockpile capacity and reduce cost spikes for siege units.

## Siege Foundry

### Role
Siege and mechanical production.

### Produces
- Cannon Battery;
- Steam Ram;
- Ironclad Walker;
- Heavy Cannon Platform.

## Iron Bastion

### Role
Heavy defensive tower.

### Features
- High armor;
- medium range cannon;
- can enter Reinforced Mode;
- weak to long-range solar artillery.

## Steam Dock

### Role
Naval production on water maps.

### Produces
- Ironclad Gunboat;
- Armored Transport;
- Furnace Barge.

## Anvil Shrine

### Role
Hero and elite tech structure.

### Unlocks
- Valen upgrades;
- Brand command upgrades;
- Rustbreaker training;
- Heart-Sealed Iron research.
