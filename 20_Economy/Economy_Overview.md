# Economy Overview

## Primary resources
- Iron
- Coal
- Sunstone
- Coral
- Coin
- Abyssal Crystal

## Economy principles
- Multiple viable expansion paths.
- Resource denial is strategic.
- Trade complements gathering.
- Rare resources unlock advanced technology.

## Faction focus
Ferrum: mining.
Aurelia: infrastructure and authority.
Thalassia: trade and ports.
Ashlands: corruption-driven resource conversion.
