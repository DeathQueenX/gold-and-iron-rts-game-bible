# Diplomacy System

## Scope
Diplomacy is primarily for campaign, custom scenarios, and large multiplayer modes.

## Core states
- War
- Truce
- Trade Pact
- Defensive Alliance
- Full Alliance
- Hostile Neutral

## Core actions
- Offer resources
- Request passage
- Open trade route
- Demand tribute
- Exchange prisoners
- Share vision

## Faction tendencies
Ferrum values military reliability and supply access.
Aurelia values authority, formal treaties, and territorial order.
Thalassia values trade rights, ports, and sea-lane access.
Ashlands does not negotiate in the standard system.

## Campaign consequences
Diplomatic choices may unlock missions, units, relics, or alternate endings.
