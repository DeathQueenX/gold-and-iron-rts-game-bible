# Aurelia Tech Tree

## Design intent

Aurelia trebuie să ofere power spikes clare prin ordine, sunstone și formații. Este o facțiune care pedepsește dezorganizarea, dar recompensează controlul armatei.

## Tier 1 — Solar Order

### Starting structures
- Solar Forum;
- Radiant Barracks;
- Sunstone Chapel;
- Imperial Road Marker.

### Available units
- Sun Mason;
- Solar Guard;
- Sun Spearman;
- Radiant Archer.

### Research
- Imperial Drills — formation bonuses improved.
- Sunstone Harvesting — Sunstone nodes gather faster.
- Road Authority — connected buildings generate more Authority.

## Tier 2 — Crystal Doctrine

### Requirements
- Sunstone Chapel;
- connected Imperial Road network;
- one Sunstone Shrine.

### Unlocked structures
- Weaver Academy;
- Crystal Yard;
- Dawn Tower.

### Available units
- Light Weaver;
- Imperial Lancer;
- Sunstone Ballista.

### Research
- Radiant Shields — Light Weavers improve wards.
- Crystal Lenses — Ballista range increased.
- Dawn Charge — Imperial Lancers gain stronger first impact.
- Purity Rites — better anti-rust and anti-corruption.

## Tier 3 — Twin Sun Ascendancy

### Requirements
- Solar Forum upgraded;
- Weaver Academy;
- Solar Mirror Array.

### Available units
- High Weaver;
- Dawnblade;
- Crystal Sentinel.

### Research
- Twin Sun Alignment — solar abilities stronger in open terrain.
- Starlight Refraction — shields reflect minor ranged damage.
- Imperial Mandate — Authority decrees last longer.
- Purging Radiance — anti-Ashlands global upgrade.

## Ultimate tech

### Mirror of Helios
Super-tech focused on vision and precision strikes.

Effects:
- temporary global reveal in a selected lane;
- improves Solar Edict and Sunstone Ballista accuracy;
- reduces corruption in controlled zones.

## Balance concerns

Aurelia must not become a pure artillery faction. It should need:

- formation protection;
- road network;
- sunstone control;
- vision;
- commanders.
