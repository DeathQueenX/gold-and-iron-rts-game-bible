# Ferrum Tech Tree

## Design intent

Ferrum trebuie să aibă o curbă de putere lentă, dar solidă. Tech tree-ul se simte ca o industrializare progresivă: de la apărare montană la mașini de război.

## Tier 1 — Survival Forge

### Starting structures
- Iron Hall;
- Forge Yard;
- Coal Silo;
- Watch Post.

### Available units
- Forge Hand;
- Ash Militia;
- Iron Guard;
- Ash Crossbowmen.

### Research
- Reinforced Tools — workers gather Iron faster.
- Ash Filters — infantry takes less damage from Ashlands weather.
- Iron Discipline — nearby units gain morale near Iron Hall.

## Tier 2 — War Foundry

### Requirements
- Great Furnace;
- 2 controlled Iron Mines or Coal Seams.

### Unlocked structures
- Siege Foundry;
- Anvil Shrine;
- Iron Bastion.

### Available units
- Forge Pike;
- Forge Engineer;
- Cannon Battery;
- Steam Ram.

### Research
- Plate Harness — +armor for infantry.
- Coal Pressure Valves — siege units reload faster.
- Reinforced Roads — heavy units move faster on Ferrum roads.
- Field Barricades — Forge Engineers unlock barricades.

## Tier 3 — Dominion Arsenal

### Requirements
- Upgraded Iron Hall;
- Great Furnace;
- Siege Foundry;
- Anvil Shrine.

### Available units
- Rustbreaker;
- Ironclad Walker;
- Heavy Cannon Platform;
- Ironclad Gunboat.

### Research
- Purified Iron — reduces rust debuff duration.
- Deep Boilers — steam units gain HP but cost more Coal.
- Magnetic Harness — unlocks magnetic abilities for elite units.
- Forge Heat Capacitors — stores Forge Heat for emergency buffs.

## Ultimate tech

### Heart-Sealed Iron
Hybrid defensive tech created for anti-Ashlands missions.

Effects:
- Ferrum mechanical units resist corruption;
- Iron Bastions emit minor cleanse pulse;
- Rustbreakers unlock Cleanse the Plate.

### Cost
- High Iron;
- High Coal;
- rare purified resource or campaign unlock.

## Balance concerns

Ferrum must not become impossible to break. Counters should remain:

- long-range magic;
- economic harassment;
- naval blockades;
- armor-ignoring damage;
- mobility and flanking.
