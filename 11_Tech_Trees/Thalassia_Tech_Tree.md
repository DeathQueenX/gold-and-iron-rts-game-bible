# Thalassia Tech Tree

## Design intent

Thalassia trebuie să aibă cel mai flexibil tech tree, dar și cea mai mare dependență de hartă. Pe hărți cu apă devine o facțiune de control economic și mobilitate. Pe hărți uscate trebuie să folosească mercenari, mist tech și trade.

## Tier 1 — Harbor Compact

### Starting structures
- Tide Hall;
- Reef Barracks;
- Whitewood Dock if water start;
- Coral Market.

### Available units
- Tidewright;
- Coral Guard;
- Tide Spearman;
- Mist Skirmisher;
- Whitewood Cutter.

### Research
- Salt Contracts — upkeep reduced for naval units.
- Coral Tools — faster construction near water.
- Harbor Signals — port vision increased.

## Tier 2 — Tidecraft

### Requirements
- Coral Market;
- controlled port or trade lane.

### Unlocked structures
- Weaver Basin;
- Mist Beacon;
- Grand Dock.

### Available units
- Water Weaver;
- Reef Engineer;
- Pearl Barge;
- Tide Galleon.

### Research
- Mist Nets — mist zones last longer.
- Reef Barriers — unlock floating blockers.
- Current Sails — ships move faster on trade lanes.
- Canal Doctrine — infantry gains speed on delta terrain.

## Tier 3 — Grand Sea Mandate

### Requirements
- upgraded Tide Hall;
- Grand Dock;
- at least two active trade lanes.

### Available units
- Pearlguard;
- Stormcaller Weaver;
- Grand Galleon;
- Tidal Siege Platform.

### Research
- Pearl Oath — port defenders gain shields.
- Deep Current Maps — reveals sea routes periodically.
- Tidal Supremacy — naval units gain combat bonus near controlled ports.
- Saltwater Ward — anti-corruption structures unlocked.

## Ultimate tech

### Crown of the Sea Roads
Economic and naval supremacy technology.

Effects:
- trade lanes generate bonus Coin;
- ships gain speed and vision;
- port captures happen faster;
- Mist Beacons link into a network.

## Balance concerns

Thalassia must not auto-win water maps. Counters:

- Ferrum coastal artillery;
- Aurelia solar mirrors;
- land pressure on ports;
- destroying trade buoys;
- dry map objectives.
