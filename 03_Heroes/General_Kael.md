# Character / Hero Candidate: General Kael

## Role
Aurelia field commander focused on aggression, cavalry timing, and solar siege support.

## RTS class
- Commander
- Burst assault
- Faction: Aurelia

## Ability concepts
- Forward Order: boosts movement toward marked objective.
- Sun at Our Backs: increases charge damage in open terrain.
- Break Their Line: weakens enemy formation bonuses.
- Imperial Assault: ultimate attack-speed and morale aura.

## Campaign use
- Border war antagonist.
- Counterpoint to Brand.
- Optional playable commander in Aurelia campaign missions.

## Voice direction
Cold, controlled, militaristic.
