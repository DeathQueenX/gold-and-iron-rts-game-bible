# Character / Hero Candidate: High Preceptor Oron

## Role
Aurelia ritual specialist focused on support, authority generation, and solar wards.

## RTS class
- Support caster
- Faction: Aurelia

## Ability concepts
- Seal of Precision: improves allied ranged accuracy.
- Preceptor's Ward: grants magic resistance.
- Written Decree: reduces upgrade time for a selected building.
- Solar Concordance: ultimate area shield and purification.

## Campaign use
- Mentor and ideological authority.
- Tutorial source for Solar Court systems.

## Voice direction
Dry, formal, restrained.
