# Hero: Tavis

## Canonical book basis

Tavis appears in *A Court of Storm and Tide*, chapter 13. He is a former scholar of the Aethelgard Academy who became a black-market alchemist in the Drowned Ring. He trades in forbidden lore, poisons and military information. His knowledge includes water-weaving physiology, Coral Gate defenses, sun-stone systems and Abyssal soul-bond theory.

## Abilities

### Desiccant Compound
Reduces mana regeneration and weakens water-based abilities on one enemy. Especially effective against Water Weavers.

### Forbidden Reagent
Applies an alchemical poison that deals light damage and reduces healing received.

### Academy Cipher
Analyzes an enemy hero, caster or magical structure. Reveals its defenses and increases damage received from the appropriate counter type.

### Thermal Collapse
Ultimate. Tavis destabilizes a target area containing water or solar-powered defenses, applying silence and damage over time. Strong against defensive grids, but weaker on ordinary ground.

## Battlefield identity

Tavis should not fight directly. He gains value by identifying enemy systems, preparing counters and disabling magical defenses before an assault. His low durability makes positioning essential.
