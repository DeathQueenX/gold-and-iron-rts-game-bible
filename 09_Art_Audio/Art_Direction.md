# Art Direction

## High-level style

Gold and Iron RTS trebuie să aibă un stil fantasy dark-industrial, lizibil de sus, dar cu personalitate cinematografică.

## Camera assumptions

- RTS camera top-down angled;
- units must be readable at small scale;
- silhouettes matter more than ornament density;
- hero units can have stronger VFX and larger silhouette.

## Faction palettes

### Ferrum
- primary: black iron, charcoal, basalt;
- secondary: orange forge glow, dark red rust;
- accent: pale steel, soot grey.

### Aurelia
- primary: ivory, gold, sunlit white;
- secondary: warm yellow, crystal amber;
- accent: violet starlight for specific characters.

### Thalassia
- primary: turquoise, deep blue, pearl white;
- secondary: coral pink, sea green;
- accent: silver foam, mist grey.

### Ashlands
- primary: ash grey, black obsidian;
- secondary: abyssal red, violet crystal;
- accent: sickly rust orange.

## Unit readability rules

- Ferrum units: blocky, heavy, broad shoulders, low center of gravity.
- Aurelia units: upright, symmetrical, bright helmets and spears.
- Thalassia units: curved armor, flowing cloth, tridents, light profiles.
- Ashlands units: asymmetrical, broken silhouettes, glowing cracks.

## Building readability rules

- Ferrum buildings should look like they can survive artillery.
- Aurelia buildings should look ordered and ceremonial.
- Thalassia buildings should look modular and maritime.
- Ashlands structures should look grown, cracked or exposed from ancient ruins.

## VFX rules

- Solar magic: clean, sharp, geometric rays.
- Iron magic: sparks, magnetic arcs, heavy shockwaves.
- Water magic: curves, spray, mist, foam, ripples.
- Abyssal magic: pulses, cracks, distortion, rust particles.

## UI style

UI should combine:

- dark metal frames for neutral RTS readability;
- faction color overlays;
- parchment/map panels for campaign;
- icon language based on resources and crests.
