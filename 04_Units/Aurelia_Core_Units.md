# Aurelia Core Units

## Worker: Sun Mason

### Role
Builder, shrine attendant, road connector.

### Abilities
- Build Aurelian structures;
- Repair crystal structures;
- Lay Imperial Road segment near controlled buildings.

## Infantry: Solar Guard

### Role
Balanced frontline infantry.

### Strengths
- formation bonuses;
- good morale;
- strong with commanders.

### Weaknesses
- weaker than Iron Guard in prolonged melee;
- vulnerable if isolated.

## Spear: Sun Spearman

### Role
Anti-cavalry and defensive line.

### Ability
**Radiant Brace** — bonus armor and anti-charge damage while stationary.

## Ranged: Radiant Archer

### Role
Precision ranged pressure.

### Ability
**Sun-Tipped Volley** — short burst of solar damage, better in daylight or near Solar Forum.

## Caster: Light Weaver

### Role
Support, shields, purification.

### Abilities
- Minor Ward;
- Purify Rust;
- Illuminate Fog.

## Elite Caster: High Weaver

### Role
Advanced caster for crowd control and anti-Abyssal gameplay.

### Abilities
- Solar Bind;
- Starlight Mirror;
- Purity Wave.

## Siege: Sunstone Ballista

### Role
Long-range crystal artillery.

### Strengths
- high precision;
- bonus against corrupted units;
- can charge shots.

### Weakness
- fragile;
- requires sunstone supply.

## Cavalry: Imperial Lancer

### Role
Fast charge and flank unit.

### Strengths
- excellent on open plains;
- can punish ranged units.

### Weakness
- poor in mountains and delta terrain;
- countered by pikes.

## Elite: Dawnblade

### Role
High-damage elite infantry.

### Ability
**Radiant Cut** — attacks apply brief armor-ignoring solar burn.

## Mechanical/Crystal: Crystal Sentinel

### Role
Late-game durable magical construct.

### Strengths
- immune to morale effects;
- strong against Abyssal creeps;
- scales with sunstone tech.

### Weakness
- expensive;
- vulnerable to siege and anti-crystal upgrades.
