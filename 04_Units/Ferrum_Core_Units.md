# Ferrum Core Units

## Worker: Forge Hand

### Role
Builder, repair unit, mine worker.

### Abilities
- Build Ferrum structures;
- Repair mechanical units;
- Stoke Forge: temporarily increases production speed at industrial buildings.

### Notes
Forge Hands should look practical and rugged, carrying tools, coal satchels and iron braces.

## Light Infantry: Ash Militia

### Role
Cheap early defense.

### Strengths
- low cost;
- good near towers;
- can be upgraded into garrison support.

### Weaknesses
- poor armor;
- weak morale without commander.

## Heavy Infantry: Iron Guard

### Role
Main frontline infantry.

### Strengths
- high armor;
- strong in chokepoints;
- benefits from Iron Anchor.

### Weaknesses
- slow;
- vulnerable to magic armor penetration.

## Anti-Cavalry: Forge Pike

### Role
Holds lines against cavalry and monsters.

### Ability
**Brace Formation** — gains bonus against charge attacks but cannot move.

## Ranged: Ash Crossbowmen

### Role
Medium ranged unit with armor-piercing bolts.

### Ability
**Black Bolt** — short cooldown shot that reduces armor temporarily.

## Support: Forge Engineer

### Role
Repairs, deploys field barricades, improves siege.

### Abilities
- Deploy Barricade;
- Emergency Repair;
- Siege Calibration.

## Siege: Cannon Battery

### Role
Long-range anti-building artillery.

### Weakness
Needs setup time and protection.

## Mechanical: Steam Ram

### Role
Gate breaker and anti-fortification push unit.

### Ability
**Boiler Charge** — brief speed burst, then overheats.

## Elite: Rustbreaker

### Role
Anti-Abyssal heavy infantry.

### Requirement
High-tier forge + purified iron upgrade.

### Ability
**Cleanse the Plate** — removes rust stacks from nearby units.

## Naval: Ironclad Gunboat

### Role
Short-range armored naval unit.

### Strength
Hard counter to light ships.

### Weakness
Slow turning, vulnerable to water-weaver displacement.
