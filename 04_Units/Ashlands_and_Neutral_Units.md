# Ashlands and Neutral Units

## Purpose

Ashlands and neutral units create map pressure, creep camps, boss objectives, and campaign escalation. They should not feel like random monsters. They should interact with corruption, terrain and resources.

## Ashlands units

### Rust Beast

**Class:** Monster / Creep

**Role:** Early-mid threat, rust debuff applicator.

**Ability:** Rust Bite — applies Rust Stack, reducing armor and repair efficiency.

**Use:** Guards small Abyssal Crystal nodes and corrupted mines.

### Ash Wraith

**Class:** Spirit / Harasser

**Role:** Vision denial and ranged disruption.

**Ability:** Ash Blindness — reduces target vision and ranged accuracy.

**Use:** Appears during ash storms and Heart Pulse events.

### Iron Husk Soldier

**Class:** Corrupted Infantry

**Role:** Former soldiers consumed by rust; slow but durable.

**Ability:** Husk March — ignores morale and minor slows.

**Use:** Campaign missions in corrupted battlefields.

### Abyssal Crawler

**Class:** Swarm Monster

**Role:** Mass creep that punishes undefended workers.

**Ability:** Burrow Ambush — hides in corrupted ground.

**Use:** Spawns from Rust Blooms.

### Star-Forge Guardian

**Class:** Ancient Guardian

**Role:** Mini-boss for ruin objectives.

**Ability:** Phase Shield — cycles resistance between physical and magical damage.

**Use:** Protects relic fragments and Star-Forge ruins.

### Abyssal Colossus

**Class:** Boss

**Role:** Major map event.

**Abilities:** Heart Pulse Stomp, Rust Aura, Obsidian Sweep.

**Use:** Requires coordinated army, siege and purification.

## Neutral human units

### Oakhaven Merchant Guard

**Class:** Neutral Mercenary

**Role:** Escort and hireable defense.

**Use:** Trade missions and neutral markets.

### Smuggler Cutthroat

**Class:** Neutral Light Infantry

**Role:** Ambush and infiltration.

**Use:** Thalassian undercity / Drowned Ring scenarios.

### Border Ranger

**Class:** Neutral Scout

**Role:** Vision and tracking.

**Use:** Can be hired from frontier camps.

### Free Miner Crew

**Class:** Neutral Worker Group

**Role:** Temporary mining boost.

**Use:** Rescue objectives, Ferrum-aligned maps.

## Neutral camp rewards

- minor resources;
- relic fragments;
- temporary mercenary contract;
- map vision;
- capturable neutral building;
- unique tech unlock in campaign.
