# Map Design Guidelines

## Goals
Each map must support meaningful strategic choices rather than simple base races.

## Competitive maps
- 1v1: 2 starting locations, 2-4 expansion zones.
- 2v2: shared center objectives.
- 3-player: triangular layout with contested center.

## Mandatory elements
- At least one high-risk relic objective.
- At least one alternate attack route.
- Natural expansion.
- One contested expansion.
- Vision objective.

## Biome templates
- Iron Mountains
- Sunlit Plains
- Southern Delta
- Thalassian Archipelago
- Northern Ashlands
- Mixed Frontier

## Neutral objectives
- Watch Towers
- Trade Posts
- Star-Forge Ruins
- Ancient Bridges
- Sunstone Shrines
- Coral Reefs

## Design rules
- No dead ends without reward.
- Every choke must have counterplay.
- Naval maps must still allow land victories.
- Large battles should naturally occur around objectives, not only bases.
